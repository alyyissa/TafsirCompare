## Exegetical Methodology

Comprehensive methodology addressing linguistic, grammatical, jurisprudential, theological, and philosophical dimensions with inclusivity toward both Shiʿa and Sunni interpretations.

## Key Contributions

- Authored **Majmaʿ al-Bayān**, one of the greatest classical Shiʿa tafsīrs
- Created inclusive methodology engaging both Shiʿa and Sunni scholarship
- Established a model of balanced and systematic Qur’anic exegesis
- Bridged sectarian boundaries through scholarly dialogue
